Austria 2 is a really good gizz-like album that I found while browsing bandcamp. Mondo previously released Ponderous Leviathan which is an insanely peak record, this one follows in suit with its really strange lyrics, titles like "glop of glue", and short-form instant head-filling fuzz that I REALLY really enjoy!!!

I think it means a lot to me now, but when the summer rolls around (it is currently march 2025) it will hit a LOT harder when I'm more mad at my job.
