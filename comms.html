Commission Information

Hello! I'm Timber, I do digital art commissions. If you're interested in one, contact me on Bluesky with your ideas for a commission!

Workflow

  • After payment, I will give a general sketch. Big revisions can be made!
  • If everything is good, I will finalize it. Minor revisions can be made!
  • If finished, I'll send uncompressed .tifs with Google Drive, including a PSD.

    • TOS and Notes

  • All my prices are in USD and are through Paypal.
  • I might not be able to draw very complicated things or things I am not comfortable with, I will let you know if this is the case!
  • Don't use my art for anything associated with the blockchain or AI!!
  • I'll give you a timeframe for the process before you commission me.
  • I won't do refunds!!! If it is something I've completely messed up on, like a very long wait time (months), then I will refund you!
